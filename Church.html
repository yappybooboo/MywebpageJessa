"Laoang Church"


St. Michael the Archangel Parish, also known as "Laoang Church" of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Catarman was established on 1768.
It is located in the Municipality of Laoang, Province of Northern Samar. The Parish Fiesta is celebrated every 29th day of September
And also Laoang Church is the center of Catholic faith in the island of Laoang
